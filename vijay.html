Nepal's Top Heritage Tourism Spots

Pashupatinath Temple

Pashupatinath is one of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in Kathmandu. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a major pilgrimage destination.

Lumbini

Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha, is an important spiritual site. The Maya Devi Temple, monasteries, and Ashokan Pillar are key attractions.

Swayambhunath Stupa

Also known as the Monkey Temple, Swayambhunath is an ancient Buddhist stupa offering panoramic views of Kathmandu and a spiritual ambiance.
